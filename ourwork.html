Home and Commercial Reinforcement

Anti-Intrusion Plus

Anti Intrusion Plus is expertly designed to prevent unauthorized access through windows. When applied to your existing glass, it reinforces the structure, ensuring the glass stays securely in place. This slows down intruders and provides crucial time for law enforcement to respond.

Anti-Intrusion

Anti Intrusion offers a slimmer yet highly effective way to reinforce your windows. While Anti Intrusion Plus provides greater strength, Anti Intrusion is more budget-friendly and still ensures a reinforced pane of glass. Both options minimize solar UV rays, contributing to lower utility costs.

Anti-Graffiti

Anti Graffiti is a thin 8-mil film that can be applied to the interior or exterior surfaces of glass. It acts as a protective barrier against scratches and vandalism while also offering energy-efficient benefits, similar to our other options.

Frost and Tint

Tint offers the most effective sun-blocking capabilities. With our tint and security film package, you can eliminate those unsightly screens that need replacing every five years, allowing your windows to stand out and shine while benefiting from enhanced security and sun protection.

Decal Installation

We ensure precise placement, smooth finishes, and bubble-free results, enhancing the visibility and appearance of your branding or messaging. Whether it's for advertising on a vehicle, decorating a window, or adding logos. This service saves you time and ensures your decals are applied with precision for a polished, professional look.

Window Frame Reinforcement

We focus on enhancing the security and durability of window structures by reinforcing the frames. This service involves adding materials like steel or other high-strength reinforcements to ensure windows can better withstand attempts at forced entry or damage.

Door Reinforcement

We team up with Night Lock to strengthen the door security, safeguarding your home and or business against break-ins. Providing a barricade to protect your valuables, product, loved ones/customers and overall safty!

Lock Reinforcement

Lock reinforcement involves strengthening the area around a door’s lock to prevent forced entry. By installing reinforced strike plates and high-grade screws, the door frame becomes more resistant to impact. This added layer of security helps protect against break-ins, providing peace of mind and enhanced durability for your home or business.
